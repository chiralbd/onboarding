# Authorship Agreement (Sign-Off) — Google Form

Build sheet for the Google Form that captures the **per-author sign-off** required before any
submission under [Publication & Authorship Policy §15](/shared/publication-authorship-policy.md#15-authorship-agreement-sign-off).
**One response per author, per manuscript.** Collect all authors' responses and keep them with
the project before submitting.

Each `###` block is one form question. Item-type tags (`[Short answer]`, `[Checkboxes — required]`,
etc.) tell the form builder which field to use; *italic* text is a builder note, not shown to
respondents. Mark every **required** question required in Form settings.

---

## Form title

CHIRAL Authorship Agreement & Sign-Off

## Form description

This form records your sign-off as an author on a specific manuscript/preprint/abstract before
submission. Authorship at CHIRAL follows the **ICMJE criteria** — all four are required, and
everyone listed must meet all four. Gift, guest, and ghost authorship are prohibited.

Read before signing:
- Publication & Authorship Policy — /shared/publication-authorship-policy.md
- ICMJE criteria — https://www.icmje.org/recommendations/browse/roles-and-responsibilities/defining-the-role-of-authors-and-contributors.html
- CRediT taxonomy — https://credit.niso.org/

*Form settings: turn on "Collect email addresses", "Response receipts", and allow editing of
responses (author order/CRediT can change up to submission). Do NOT limit to 1 response — an
author signs once per manuscript, so the same person may submit for several works.*
