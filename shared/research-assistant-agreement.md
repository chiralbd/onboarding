# Research Assistant Onboarding Agreement — Google Form

This file is a **build sheet** for a Google Form. Each `###` block is one form question.
Item-type tags (`[Short answer]`, `[Checkboxes — required]`, etc.) tell the form builder which
field to use. Wording that a respondent sees is in plain text; builder notes are *italic*.

Copy the **Form title** and **Form description** into the top of a new Google Form, then add one
question per `###` block. Mark every question tagged **required** as required in Form settings.

---

## Form title

CHIRAL Research Assistant — Onboarding Agreement

## Form description

This agreement records your acceptance of CHIRAL's policies as a Research Assistant (RA) and
your mandatory disclosures. It applies to **CHIRAL only** (the research wing of the DeepBio
ecosystem). It is **not** an employment contract — RA positions are unpaid — but the
commitments below are binding for as long as you are a member.

Before completing this form, read:
- Center Guidelines — /shared/center-guidelines.md
- Publication & Authorship Policy — /shared/publication-authorship-policy.md
- Recruitment Policy — /shared/recruitment-policy.md
- Data Governance Policy — /shared/data-governance.md
- Good Practices — /shared/good-practices.md
- Social Media Policy — /shared/social-media-policy.md
