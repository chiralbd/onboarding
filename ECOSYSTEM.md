# The DeepBio Ecosystem

This repository covers onboarding for **CHIRAL**, the research wing. CHIRAL is one of three
arms of a single ecosystem. This page explains the whole picture and where this repo fits.

## The three arms

| Arm | Role | Nature of membership | Onboarding lives in |
|-----|------|----------------------|---------------------|
| **DeepBio Limited** | Startup providing bioinformatics / data services | **Paid employment** (company staff, contracts) | *DeepBio Limited handbook (separate repo)* |
| **CHIRAL** — Center for Health Innovation, Research, Action, and Learning | **Research wing** | **Voluntary, unpaid** research & training positions; credit through authorship | **This repository** |
| **DeepBio Academy** | Training platform | **Trainees / students** (enrollment, curriculum) | *DeepBio Academy handbook (separate repo)* |

## Scope of this repository

- This repo's policies — [Center Guidelines](/shared/center-guidelines.md),
  [Publication & Authorship Policy](/shared/publication-authorship-policy.md), the
  voluntary-position / minimum-commitment / mandatory-affiliation rules, and the
  unit overlays — apply to **CHIRAL only**.
- **They do not govern DeepBio Limited employees or DeepBio Academy trainees.** Those arms
  have their own handbooks because their membership models differ (paid employment;
  enrollment). In particular, the *voluntary, unpaid* and *authorship-contingent* clauses are
  **research-specific** and must **not** be applied to DeepBio Limited staff.

## What is shared vs. arm-specific

Some standards are intended to be **ecosystem-wide** and are expected to read the same across
all three handbooks (kept here as the reference copy until a shared location is set up):

- Code of Conduct
- Research & professional ethics
- [Data Governance](/shared/data-governance.md) (health-data handling)
- [Social Media Policy](/shared/social-media-policy.md) — *except* the CHIRAL-specific
  mandatory-affiliation clause

Arm-specific (do **not** carry across):

- **CHIRAL:** voluntary positions, minimum-term commitment, authorship rules, lab-unit overlays.
- **DeepBio Limited:** employment terms, compensation, IP assignment, client confidentiality.
- **DeepBio Academy:** enrollment, curriculum, fees, certification.
